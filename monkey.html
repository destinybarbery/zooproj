Monkey

Like humans monkeys are very social animals. They use grooming as relationship builders, kind of like how your parents would help you do your hair. They also can live up to 50 years! Monkeys are very active animals who love to climb and jump, some can jump 15 feet, isnt that crazy? Although monkeys are cute, they are very apprehensive to humans unless they are raised around them, so be careful if you see one on the street! Dont worry though, our monkeys are very sweet!
